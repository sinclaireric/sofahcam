The tile as a floor covering is interesting in several respects, especially with regard to its composition. Indeed, it can be made of ceramic, terracotta, earthenware, or sandstone, but also marble or cement. This multitude of choices allows a great personalization, not only by the colors and other patterns that can be silk-screened, but also by the aspect released, and this because of its composition. The multitude of colors, ornaments and illustrations, combined with the different compositions available, offer an infinite number of combinations capable of meeting all requirements.

This covering is timeless, pleasant because it provides great freedom of personalization. Only the imagination limits the look that can be given to it. Finally, because of its flexibility, tile is very easy to match in a room. Whether you choose indoor or outdoor tiling, you can also choose the installation technique best suited to your needs. Finally, tile is relatively resistant to marks, scratches and other breakage. Unlike wood or parquet, it does not move over time and is not subject to insect attack. If the minimum precautions are respected, it will keep all its brightness.

The solidity of the tile gives it insulating properties. Thus, it can also be chosen for its qualities relative to both sound and heat insulation. It is therefore naturally found its place in the kitchen, especially because it is non-flammable, and in the bathrooms.
