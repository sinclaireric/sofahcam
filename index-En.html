Building a House, Brick or Cement blocks: Advantages and Disadvantages

Thermal insulation: the agglo or Cement block is less insulating than the brick, which forces the builder to implement an insulation to ensure the comfort of the building;
In Resistances : agglo or Cement block is more resistant than brick;
In terms of Price : brick is more expensive than agglo; Bricks are not suitable for long walls and does not bind with morter nor concrete for a long period of time.
